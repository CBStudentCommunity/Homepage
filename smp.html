Come join fellow Students on the Colonel By Minecraft Server!

Mine, Craft, Build, Fight and more on the Colonel By Minecraft Server!

The Colonel By Minecraft server is a semi-vanilla survival multiplayer server for members of the CBSC.

Here you can team up and hang out with fellow students here, hanging out and building in the safe zone, or making your mark in a dangerous anarchy pvp world!

Build

Create a base, grind for gear, search for hidden relics or rare structures

Play Minecraft your way

Upgrade

No matter how you play, there is always a way to improve your house, your gear and your alliances with a vibrant community

Explore

Minecraft worlds are always vast and amazing, and we choose the world we're using just for that purpose. Full of mountain ranges, vallies and rivers, there is no limit to interesting terrain to explore

Socialize

Whether peaceful or combative, there are many ways to interact with fellow students

Join

If you want to join or learn more about the CBSC Minecraft server, check out the Discord server!
